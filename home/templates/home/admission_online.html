Online Admission

St. John offers online Admission, To encourage people for online learning. so that you could attend classes from around the world.

Why Students should take Admission in Online?

  • Efficiency- Teachers can effectively give lessons to students through online learning. Teachers can use all of the resources available for online learning, including podcasts, PDFs, and videos, as part of their lesson preparations. Teachers may become more effective instructors by expanding the lesson plan beyond standard textbooks to include internet resources.
  • Accessibility Of Time And Place- The ability for students to take classes from any place of their choosing is another benefit of online education. Additionally, it frees schools from the limitations imposed by physical limits and enables them to connect with a wider network of pupils. Online lectures can also be recorded, saved, and distributed for later use. This enables students to access the instructional materials whenever it is convenient for them.
  • Suits A Variety Of Learning Styles- Every learner has a unique learning path and a unique learning style. Some students learn best through visuals, while others learn best through sounds. Similar to this, some students excel in the classroom while others study best on their own and become easily distracted by huge groups.

Utilizing technology, online learning simulates or enhances conventional teaching techniques. Some of these teaching techniques, such as using free apps to practise writing Chinese characters, will be introduced to you. You'll get the chance to test out some advances in teaching and learning made available through online learning, building on what you learned the previous week.
