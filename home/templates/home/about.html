About US:

St. John International online Autonomous university is an Autonomous body that is imparting Online Education, Distance Education, Regular & Evening college to empower people irrespective of Age, Cast, Creed and Religion for those who have lost the opportunity at the right age due to Poverty, Social Compulsion, and family conditions.

Drawing students and faculty from across India and abroad, the University has emerged as a symbol of excellence, integrity and openness of mansa (thought), vaacha (speech) and karmana (action).

All the Courses offered by St. John International university are Non-Conventional, Need Based and Tailor made model to suit the needs of the People aimed at gainful employment based on the Prime Minister's "SKILL MISSION Programme"
