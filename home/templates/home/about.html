Our Mission

Founded in 1870, St. John’s is a Catholic and Vincentian university that prepares students for personal and professional success in today’s global society and to provide an opportunity to deliver a Lifelong Learning, Flexible learning process and achieve the Life Goals of the people irrespective of their age or qualification who otherwise could not attend colleges or Academies due to various compulsions.
