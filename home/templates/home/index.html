About St. John University

At St. John University, you can have it all: a Catholic and Vincentian education that broadens your mind, the opportunity to serve your community, the resources and excitement of New York City and our global locations, and the support and network you need for success.It is an Autonomous body that is imparting "ONLINE EDUCATION" to empower people irrespective of Age, Cast, Creed and Religion for those who have lost the opportunity at the right age due to Poverty, Social Compulsion, and family conditions.

Graphic Design Graduate Uses Art to Inspire Social Change

Millena Cecilia Santos Albuquerque ’22C from Harrison, NJ, believes she found a calling during her four years in the Graphic Design program in St. John College of Liberal Arts and Sciences. Outfitted now with a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree and a body of public work, Millena looks forward to a meaningful future as an artist/advocate in the line of Banksy, Paula Champagne, and Deva Pardue.

Alumna Leads Eco-Friendly TV Production Studio in Brooklyn

Gina Argento ’94C, ’96G rooted for the Red Storm while earning her bachelor’s and master’s degrees at St. John University. Along the way, she helped improve the planet through “green” initiatives at her company, Broadway Stages, Ltd., one of the film industry’s few eco-friendly soundstages.
